Patek Philippe Golden Ellipse reference 3738 in condition like new. This iconic model in 18-carat yellow gold embodies the timeless elegance of the Ellipse collection, first introduced in 1968 and based on the principles of the “Golden Ratio.” Often referred to as the “Jumbo,” the reference 3738 offers the perfect balance of presence and refinement with its 35.5 x 31 mm case. The distinctive blue sunburst dial with slender applied indices highlights the pure, graphic clarity of this legendary design. Inside beats the ultra-thin automatic in-house caliber 240, celebrated for its technical sophistication and reliability. With its harmonious case shape and its historical importance within the Patek Philippe collection, the 3738 is a sought-after collector’s piece that uniquely bridges modernity and classicism. A masterpiece of design and watchmaking that has set benchmarks for decades.

"divine" proportion

Ellipse d’Or

The design of the ellipse was inspired by the principle of the "golden ratio" discovered by the mathematicians of Greek antiquity. This "divine" proportion, 1:1.6181, is the basis for many famous buildings and works of art in history and also that of the Ellipse d'Or.
